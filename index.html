Dungeons & Dragons 5e

Fridays, 5:30 p.m., online

The classic. The game that launched a thousand imitations. Don't be intimidated by the rulebook - there's more than enough room for goofing around and telling incredible stories here.

Mondays, 7:00 p.m., Archibald's Coffee

Similar to D&D in many ways, though the rules can be a bit more complicated. This game allows for a more flexible playstyle.

Monster of the Week

Thursdays, 6:00 p.m., Brecker's Pub

Camp and horror and fantasy combine at the game table here. Lighter on the rules, heavier on the roleplaying elements than some other games. Think Buffy, but with your friends.
